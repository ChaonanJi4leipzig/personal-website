Bio

Post-doctoral Researcher at Leipzig University, Germany. My research focuses on remote sensing, climate extremes, and ecosystem science, with expertise in hyperspectral image classification, time-series analysis, and machine learning for Earth observation.

Research Interests

  • Remote sensing – hyperspectral image classification (laboratory, airborne, spaceborne), time-series analysis, multispectral–hyperspectral data integration, urban heat-island studies.
  • Climate extremes – detection of compound heat-wave and drought events; forecasting vegetation impacts.
  • Ecosystem science – assessment of vegetation condition, dynamics, resistance, resilience; plant-trait retrieval from hyperspectral data.
  • Techniques – Earth-system data-cube generation; machine-learning forecasting models.
